Jaisalmer Travel Packages

Royal Jaisalmer Explorer: 2D/2N Desert Odyssey

Immerse yourself in the golden sands and rich heritage of Jaisalmer with our comprehensive 2-day, 2-night package that covers all the must-visit destinations.

Day 1: Historical Exploration

  • Morning: Jaisalmer Fort Tour Explore the magnificent Golden Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Afternoon: Patwon Ki Haveli & Salim Singh Ki Haveli Visit the intricate architectural marvels of Jaisalmer's famous havelis
  • Evening: Gadisar Lake Sunset boat ride and photography session at the historic lake
  • Night: Cultural Evening at Khuri Village Traditional Rajasthani folk dance, music, and bonfire

Day 2: Desert Adventures

  • Morning: Sam Sand Dunes Camel safari, desert photography, and sunrise experience
  • Afternoon: Tanot Mata Temple & Longewala War Memorial Historical site visit commemorating India's military courage
  • Evening: Kuldhara Abandoned Village Explore the mysterious ghost village with fascinating legends
  • Night: Desert Camping & Cultural Night Stargazing, traditional Rajasthani dinner, and folk performances

  • Accommodation at T. S. Haveli
  • All local transportation
  • Professional English-speaking guide
  • Camel safari

  • Entry fees to monuments
